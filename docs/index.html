Privacy Policy for Tibeb

Effective Date: 2026-07-02

This Privacy Policy applies to the Tibeb mobile application (the “Application”), developed by Samuel A. (“Service Provider”) as an open-source, free-to-use reading companion.

Tibeb is designed with privacy at its core. Our philosophy is simple: your reading belongs to you.

1. Overview

Tibeb is built to help users read, listen, and track their reading journey. The Application is designed to function locally on your device and does not require user accounts or personal identification.

2. Data Collection

Tibeb does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.

  • No account or registration is required
  • No email or personal data is collected
  • No reading content is uploaded to external servers
  • No tracking or advertising SDKs are used

All reading data (such as books, progress, highlights, and settings) is stored locally on your device.

3. Device Permissions

The Application may request limited device permissions solely for core functionality, such as:

  • Access to local storage (to open books)
  • Audio playback (for audiobooks)

These permissions are used only on-device and are never shared externally.

4. Third-Party Services

Tibeb does not integrate advertising networks, analytics platforms, or third-party tracking services.

If optional open-source libraries are used, they are selected carefully to preserve user privacy.

5. Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, you maintain full control over your information. Uninstalling the Application will permanently remove all locally stored data.

6. Children’s Privacy

Tibeb does not knowingly collect any personal data from users of any age, including children under 13.

We encourage parents and guardians to supervise digital reading activities where appropriate.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.

Continued use of the Application after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Your Rights

Since Tibeb does not collect personal data, there is no stored personal information to access, modify, or delete on external systems.

You may delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the Application.
